Inspiration for NokuSocial

The social giants have grown up to challenge governments, people's rights and work with private group of companies to control flow of information. There is no point in using a social media if truth is suppressed. Free speech protections endowed to us by constitution do not protect individuals from censorship by a private entity, whether it’s from a church, employer or social media company. This gives companies like Twitter and Facebook the right to create their own rules that can restrict the speech of its users in almost any way it sees fit. Thus main stream social media platforms have turned into an unbearable fascist platform within past decade. People need to step up and give befitting reply to this tyrannical social media corporates. This is why we are going to build nokusocial.

"In the age of rampant censorship, nokusocial will be people's voice and freedom"

Roadmap

*roadmap vector by vecteezy

Stage - I

Starts from developmental phase of project and continues upto a year from date of release of noku. During this time noku will be undergoing test phase with active development to address issues, drawbacks, bugs and implementation of new features. This will be a phase of growth and development of platform. Most of crowdfunding goes to this stage.

Stage - II

One year after launch of noku, the project shifts to phase II. Next one year will execute further development and quest to achieve goals of noku platform. 
The major goals include
• Establish an independent powerful server with high volume of storage support
• Upscaling of project team to address upcoming challenges
• Develop mobile apps for IOs and android

Stage - III

Once stage two is complete, nokusocial would enter into stage III. In this stage nokusocial becomes sustainable and stable. 

How to make this project real?

The question that now comes is how does NokuSocial is going to to be built or become sustainable without any usual economic models. Our power comes from support people give. Since we don't intent to run any complex algorithms or Artificial Intelligence (AI) for targeted ads, we don't have a money trailer or greedy pattern of business to follow. This helps us to have reasonable expenses and follow a very affordable economic model for you. We rely on strength in numbers and simplicity. So this project entirely relies on you and is intended to serve your need for a fair online social media system. Once funded by community, noku will be deployed for people to join and start growing as a community. The economic plan for each stage is outlined in the image given below. This is a proposed economic model and is an ideal one. There may arise minute changes when the project progresses but any changes will be in people's favor and for better sustainability.

What's good about this model?

  • PRINCIPLE OF OWING TO PEOPLE - This model ensures that the sole survival of platform relies entirely on people. This in turn encourages platform to respect users opinion and safeguard user's rights, something that free platforms often fails to offer. A negligible subscription also makes nokusocial, a software as a service platform. So nokusocial is obliged to serve people's interest unlike the free platforms that is run in accordance with the likenesses of the biggest investors. 
  • LOW BURDEN - The plan is to reduce amount spent to 1$ per year, ensure your freedom of speech and protect your rights. This is a small price to pay to keep nokusocial alive and serve people. We stand with people as we are made by people.
  • SAFER PLATFORM - A small monetized system provide a bottle neck on spammers and numerous fake accounts.
