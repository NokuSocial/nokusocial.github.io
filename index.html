Roadmap

Phase I

Phase I begins from the crowdfunding phase and can almost continue up to a year from the date of release. During this time, Noku will be undergoing test phases with active development to address issues, drawbacks, bugs, and implementation of new features. This will be a substantial phase of growth and development, and most crowdfunding donations will be invested into this stage.

Phase - II

One year post-launch of Noku, the project will shift to Phase II. During this period, we will focus on further development and achieve the core goals of NokuSocial.
These goals include:
Establish an independent powerful server with high volume of storage support
Upscaling of project team to address upcoming challenges
Develop mobile apps for iOS and Android

Phase - III

Once Phase II is complete, NokuSocial will become sustainable and stable, with minor bug fixes and changes via community requests. It will encompass our ongoing commitment to serving you as a consumer, enabling a safe, fast, secure, and connective experience on NokuSocial.

How to make this project real?

Our platform will be solely fueled by support of our users and community. We rely on strength in numbers and simplicity. This said, we need your support to make this platform a reality.

Since we don't intend to run any complex algorithms or Artificial Intelligence (AI) for targeted ads, we don't have a money trailer or greedy pattern of business to follow. This helps us to have reasonable expenses and follow a very affordable economic model for you.

Once funded by our supporters, NokuSocial will be launched for people to join and start growing as a community. See our stage-by-stage economic plan below. 

What's good about this model?

  • PRINCIPLE OF OWING TO PEOPLE - This model ensures that the survival of NokuSocial relies entirely on people. This ensures you that your opinion and safeguard will be our highest priority, something that other non-costly platforms often fail to offer. A small subscription also makes NokuSocial a software, in addition to a service platform. NokuSocial is obliged to serve the people, unlike other platforms that are run in accordance with large investors.
  • LOW BURDEN - The plan is to reduce our support request to $1 per year, while ensuring your freedom of speech and protecting your rights. This is a small price to pay to keep NokuSocial alive and serve free speech. We stand with you and for your rights.
  • SAFER PLATFORM - Our low-cost monthly subscription provides safety and security against possible spammers and fake accounts
